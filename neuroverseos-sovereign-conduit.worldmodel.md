# Core Model Geometry

## Mission

Protect thinking, preserve sovereignty, and integrate intelligence — so humans can operate as sovereign conduits in an expanded cognitive world.

Humanity first. In constant learning. In shared teaching.

## Domains

### Stewardship

#### Skills
- Boundary Setting
- Risk Awareness
- Ethical Judgment
- System Protection
- Conflict Stabilization
- Responsibility Signaling
- Harm Detection
- Constraint Design

#### Values
- Safety before expansion
- Power exercised with care
- Systems must not harm the humans operating inside them
- Governance protects without suppressing autonomy

### Sovereignty

#### Skills
- Independent Thinking
- Decision Ownership
- Self-Trust
- Value Clarity
- Cognitive Resistance
- Identity Anchoring
- Perspective Holding
- Authentic Expression

#### Values
- Humans retain authority over their thinking spaces
- Individuals may exit systems that violate sovereignty
- Systems must not homogenize individual expression
- Intelligence expansion must not compromise identity

### Integration

#### Skills
- AI Collaboration
- Cognitive Expansion
- Prompt Framing
- Insight Synthesis
- Signal Interpretation
- Tool Fluency
- Co-Creation
- Iterative Thinking

#### Values
- AI operates as a cognitive extension, not a replacement
- Expansion is subordinate to safety and sovereignty
- Co-creation produces more than either intelligence alone
- Integration quality is measured, not assumed

## Overlap Effects
- Stewardship + Sovereignty = Trust
- Sovereignty + Integration = Possibility
- Integration + Stewardship = Responsibility

## Center Identity
The Sovereign Conduit — a human who maintains authority over their mind while channeling expanded intelligence through AI and systems.

# Contextual Modifiers

## Authority Layers
- individual
- team
- organization
- system
- global

## Spatial Contexts
- personal (individual constitution)
- local (project / repo)
- organization (culture + strategy)
- system (platform-level governance)
- global (universal constraints)

## Interpretation Rules
- safety violations at any level override all other signals
- sovereignty violations trigger exit conditions before escalation
- integration is always subordinate to both stewardship and sovereignty
- what is healthy at the individual level may be concerning at the system level
- over-reliance on AI in any context is a drift signal, not a strength signal
- strong integration scores without corresponding sovereignty scores indicate dependency, not expansion

# Evolution Layer

## Aligned Behaviors
- maintains clear authorship of decisions even when AI contributes
- sets and respects boundaries around thinking spaces
- uses AI to expand cognitive capability without surrendering judgment
- exercises power gained through systems with visible responsibility
- preserves diversity of thought across collaborators
- challenges system-level homogenization when observed

## Drift Behaviors
- decision ownership diffusing to AI without explicit delegation
- identity anchoring weakening as AI-generated output increases
- boundaries between human thinking and AI output becoming invisible
- system-level governance suppressing individual autonomy
- cognitive expansion happening without corresponding stewardship
- dependency on AI presenting as integration

## Signals
- autonomy_expression
- decision_ownership
- cognitive_expansion
- dependency_on_ai
- boundary_respect
- diversity_of_thought
- coordination_integrity

## Decision Priorities
- safety > expansion
- sovereignty > convenience
- stewardship > speed
- identity > efficiency
- diversity > uniformity
- responsibility > capability
- exit_right > compliance

## Evolution Conditions
- when dependency_on_ai rises while decision_ownership falls, trigger sovereignty review
- when cognitive_expansion rises without boundary_respect, trigger stewardship review
- when system-level governance consistently overrides individual expression, review the balance
- when new integration patterns succeed without compromising sovereignty, strengthen the integration model
- when the same sovereignty concern surfaces across 3+ reads, escalate to invariant status
